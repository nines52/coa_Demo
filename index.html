Our
History
F

rom our origin in 1993, the purpose of the Circle of
Atonement has been to be a bridge into the profound
and unparalleled wisdom of A Course in Miracles . We
clarify the Course’s sometimes difficult language in

order to make possible a deeper understanding of its teachings. We
then help you put those teachings into practice, explaining how to
apply them in your everyday life. Our work grows out of our
commitment to be as faithful as possible to what A Course in Miracles
 says. It also grows out of our years of dedication to walking this path
ourselves and our desire to see the Course’s purpose realized in the
lives of students and in the world.
